About KenYen

Who we are

Founded in 2011 by Erwin C. Laurel, KenYen Steel Works & Construction Services is a family-grown firm delivering structural steel fabrication, engineering detailing, certified welding and complete site execution. We operate with high standards of safety, regulatory compliance and client satisfaction.

Mission

Provide safe, high-quality, affordable steel fabrication and construction through stewardship, innovation and environmental responsibility.

Vision

To be the top provider of innovative, sustainable construction and steel solutions in Panabo City and beyond — setting new standards for quality, safety and client satisfaction.
