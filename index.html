About KenYen

KenYen Steel Works and Construction Services was founded in 2011 by Erwin C. Laurel. We are a family-grown company focused on structural steel fabrication, certified welding, engineering detailing and turnkey construction delivery across Panabo City and neighboring provinces. We deliver projects that meet contract specifications, regulatory requirements and industry best practice.

Mission

Providing safe, high-quality and affordable steel fabrication through stewardship, innovation and commitment to environmental responsibility.

Vision

To become the leading provider of innovative, sustainable construction and steel solutions in Panabo City and beyond — setting new standards for quality, safety and client satisfaction.
