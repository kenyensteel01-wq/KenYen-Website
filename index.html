Introduction

KenYen Steel Works and Services is a trusted name in Panabo City, recognized for delivering high-quality construction and steelwork solutions. With a strong commitment to safety and client satisfaction, we provide reliable services that meet the needs of both residential and commercial projects.

Mission

Providing safe, high-quality, and affordable steel fabrication through stewardship, innovation, and a commitment to environmental responsibility.

Vision

To become the top provider of innovative, sustainable construction and steel solutions in Panabo City and beyond. We aim to set new standards for quality, safety, and client satisfaction while fostering lasting relationships through excellence and teamwork.

Key Objectives

  • Provide top-tier construction solutions with a focus on safety, efficiency, and innovation.
  • Ensure consistent client satisfaction through excellence in service delivery.
  • Promote sustainable growth across all operations.
  • Strengthen our presence and contribute to the development of Panabo City.

Our History

"KenYen Steel Works and Construction Services" was founded in year 2011 by Mr. Erwin C. Laurel. This was built up due to the skills and passion for architecture by the owner. He was able to put up this business as a family-owned firm. The said business has done ample and numerous construction services in the locality of Panabo and even in other neighboring cities.
